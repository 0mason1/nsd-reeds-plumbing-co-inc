Comprehensive Plumbing Services

Emergency Plumbing Repairs

Burst pipes, severe leaks, and sudden failures don't wait for business hours. Our team responds fast to protect your home from water damage — day or night.

Drain Cleaning & Unclogging

About Us

Charlotte's Most Trusted Plumbing Company

Reeds Plumbing Co Inc has been a cornerstone of the Charlotte plumbing community for over three decades. Founded on the principles of honest work, fair pricing, and genuine care for every customer, we've grown from a small family operation into one of the most recognized names in the region.

Every technician on our team is fully licensed, background-checked, and committed to treating your home with the same respect they'd show their own. We show up on time, explain everything clearly, and never surprise you with hidden fees.
